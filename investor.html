Phase 2: Freight Comparison

Full Container Load (FCL) Costs: Attempting direct import natively requires leasing a 20ft container ($5,000 - $8,000), clearing customs brokerage (~$1,200), and paying for port drayage and holding facilities, totaling $8,000 to $10,000+ in fixed logistical overhead.

Bridge Importer Model (Our Execution): By utilizing importers like Cafe Imports or Genuine Origin as "Bridge" logistics providers, we purchase our specific 10–15 bag microlots from the farmer, and the importer consolidates our bags onto their existing container. They charge a fractional premium of roughly $1.00–$1.50 per pound, completely removing the $10k container execution risk from our startup.
