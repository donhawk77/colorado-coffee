4-Phase Outreach Strategy

Phase 1: The Digital Handshake

Instead of corporate vendor emails, we bypass the noise by initiating direct relationships via Instagram with smallholder farmers (e.g., @fincasoledad), transitioning them to WhatsApp where real negotiations occur.

Phase 2: The "Bridge" Importer

We avoid crushing maritime freight costs by executing the transaction via a bridge importer (like Cafe Imports or Qima Coffee). We secure the lot direct from the farmer, but the importer consolidates our 15 bags onto their shipping container for a fractional fee.

Phase 3: The Vault Value Pitch

We win allocations over massive roasteries by sending the farmer our digital architecture. We prove that APEX SG will highlight their name, their processing data, and pay 5% of gross back to environmental corridors—elevating their product, not just treating it as a commodity blend.

Phase 4: Physical Connection

We cement the contract at the annual Specialty Coffee Association (SCA) Expo, shaking hands with the producers, cupping the harvest, and generating authentic photo/video evidence for the APEX Heritage Archive.
