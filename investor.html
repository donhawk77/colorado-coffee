Attempting direct import natively requires leasing a massive 20ft container, clearing customs brokerage, and paying port holding facilities—triggering a catastrophic fixed logistical overhead.

Bridge Importer Model (Our Execution): By utilizing importers like Cafe Imports or Genuine Origin as "Bridge" logistics providers, we purchase our specific 10–15 bag microlots from the farmer, and the importer consolidates our bags onto their existing container. They charge a fractional premium of roughly $1.25 per pound, completely removing the massive container execution risk from our startup.
